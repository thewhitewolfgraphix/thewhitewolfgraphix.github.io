White Wolf Graphix

Back Story:

Formally known as Devil Designs was rebranded as White Wolf Graphix being brought back as a new company. DD Was started back in 2019 by Witcher aka The White Wolf. After a year of lockdown Witcher decided to take a break and rethink he role and views of the company.

March 31st 2021 Witcher decided to rebrand the company as White Wolf Graphix.

Vision:

Witcher's vision for the company is to bring graphix to a new level. Top level designs for a good price. Wants to leave a life time legacy behind for everyone to enjoy for years to come. Change was needed for the company. Time for a new look and new features and new content. With the new change it will change the land scape of graphics.
